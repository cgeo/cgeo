About This Item

The Golden Lisa is part of a limited collectors edition of all Simpsons characters. I received the collectors-set as a birthday-present from my friends.

Now I visited the USA for the first time and decided to leave Lisa here for a journey to her home town.
I released her in Florida during my vacation in September 2011 and hope she will travel safely across the USA towards Springfield and maybe finally end up in Germany to get home to me.
