Current GOAL

Lisa shall visit her home-town Springfield in the USA (DONE !)
As no one knows which Springfield is home of the Simpsons, she would be glad just to visit one of them.
It would be nice to see some photos from the locations and people she meets on her way.

After she reached Springfield she would like to go back to Germany to her owner (Current Mission !)

Update (April,29th 2012): Thanks to fellow cachers Lisa reached Springfield today !!

Current Mission:  
Return Home to GERMANY (Duesseldorf) !

Destination cache near the airport could be: GC3B7PD

Although she is not really made of gold she is quite valueable for me.
So try to take care of her as much as you can and do only send her to nice mugglefree places.
It's no problem to keep her some weeks unitl you find a new suitable home.

About This Item

The Golden Lisa is part of a limited collectors edition of all Simpsons characters. I received the collectors-set as a birthday-present from my friends.

Now I visited the USA for the first time and decided to leave Lisa here for a journey to her home town.
I released her in Florida during my vacation in September 2011 and hope she will travel safely across the USA towards Springfield and maybe finally end up in Germany to get home to me.

Thanks for helping Lisa to reach her first goal goes to:

1. VicknRandy (Florida -> Kansas)
2. Yortdaddy (Kansas)
3. Lee! (Kansas -> Missouri)
4. Hillbillyfun (-> Springfield)

Thanks for helping Lisa on her way back to Germany goes to:
